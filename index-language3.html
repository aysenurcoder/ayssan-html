ABOUT US

The limits of your language
means the limits of your world

1993

The school was founded in 1993,
in New York and was one of the first
in NY state.

BeSpeak is an intensive language journey for children and youth from 3 years old. It abounds in numerous adventures with music, dance, theater, rhyming poetry, art and multimedia projects.

It engages all the senses the children's imagination. It shows the fascinating world of unknown words and expressions that thanks to our methods are easy to understand and assimilate.

This is the best way to fluent language.
Enjoy an educational journey from the BeSpeak.
